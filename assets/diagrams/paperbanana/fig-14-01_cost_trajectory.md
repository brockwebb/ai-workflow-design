# Figure: Cost Trajectory -- AI Pipeline vs. Manual Process

## Diagram Type
Line chart. Two lines showing cumulative cost over time. Clean, minimal, professional.

## Axes
X-axis: Years (0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5). Label: "Year"
Y-axis: Cumulative cost in dollars. Label: "Cumulative Cost"
Y-axis range: $0 to $120,000. Use $20,000 increments.

## Lines

### Line 1: AI Pipeline
Label: "AI Pipeline"
Style: solid line, blue or teal
Data points:
- Year 0: $3,500 (development cost, one-time)
- Year 1: $3,515 ($3,500 development + $15 first run)
- Year 2: $3,530
- Year 3: $3,545
- Year 4: $3,560
- Year 5: $3,575
Annotation near Year 2: "Year 2+: ~$15/cycle"

### Line 2: Manual Process
Label: "Manual Process"
Style: dashed line, orange or red
Data points:
- Year 0: $800 (one-time training)
- Year 1: $24,000 ($800 training + $23,200 first cycle)
- Year 2: $47,200
- Year 3: $70,400
- Year 4: $93,600
- Year 5: $116,800
Annotation near Year 3: "$23,200/cycle"

## Crossover / Break-even
Draw a vertical dashed line at approximately Year 1 where the gap becomes large.
Label: "Break-even within Year 1"

## Visual Style
- Clean, sparse, publication-quality
- White or very light background
- Light gray gridlines
- Bold labels for both lines
- No decorative elements
- Sans-serif font
- Legend in upper left

## Caption
"Cumulative cost comparison: AI pipeline vs. manual process over five years. Development cost is recovered within the first cycle. The gap compounds annually."
